Frequently Asked Questions

🚗 How does self-drive work?▼
You pick a car, we deliver it to your doorstep, and you drive it yourself. No driver needed — just your license and a sense of adventure. Return it when you're done!
🚚 Is delivery really free?▼
Yes! We deliver and pick up the vehicle anywhere in Colombo at no extra charge. Free doorstep service, every time.

Absolutely. Every rental includes comprehensive insurance coverage so you can drive with complete peace of mind.

Every rental includes 150km free. Extra km are charged at just LKR 60/km. No hidden fees, no surprises.
