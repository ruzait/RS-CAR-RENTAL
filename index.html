Frequently Asked Questions

🚗 How does self-drive work?▼
You pick a car, we deliver it free anywhere in the Eastern Province, and you drive it yourself. No driver needed — just your license and a sense of adventure. Return it when you're done!
🚚 Is delivery really free?▼
Yes! We deliver and pick up the vehicle anywhere in the Eastern Province at no extra charge. Free doorstep service, every time.
🛡️ Is the car insured?▼
Absolutely. Every rental includes comprehensive insurance coverage so you can drive with complete peace of mind.
🎁 What about km limits for first-time customers?▼
60km free per rental for first-time customers. Extra km charges depend on the vehicle fees. No hidden fees, no surprises.
📞 How do I make a booking?▼
Call us, send a WhatsApp message, or fill out the contact form. We typically respond within 30 minutes.
